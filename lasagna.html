Easy Lasagna

A quick and easy way to make delicious lasagna

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces lasagna noodles
  • 1 1\2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 (6 ounces) can tomato paste

Step 1

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

Step 2

In a skillet, brown ground beef. Using a fork, mix cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, parsley, salt and pepper. In a separate bowl combine tomato paste and tomato sauce.

Step 3

In a 9x13 inch baking pan, layer noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixture and cottage chees mixture. Two layers of each will fit. Cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove tin foil the last 8 minutes of baking time.

Per Serving: 491 calories; protein 38g; carbohydrates 39.7g; fat 20.3g; cholesterol 126.4mg; sodium 926.6mg.
