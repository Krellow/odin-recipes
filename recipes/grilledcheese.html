Quick and Easy Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon butter, softened
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of sharp Chedder Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill

Directions

  • Step 1

    • Spread 1/2 tablespoon of butter on one side of each piece of bread. Lie the slices of Cheddar on one of the slices of bread on the unbuttered side. Sprinkle the parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, and dill on the unbuttered side. Sandwich the two sides of bread together with the buttered sides facing outwards.

  • Step 2

    • Heat a skillet over medium heat. When skillet is hot, gently lie the sandwich in the skillet; cook on each side for 3 minutes until cheese has melted.

Nutriton Facts

Per Serving:471 calories; protien 18.4g; carbohydrates 27.4g; gat 32.2g; cholestrol 90.1mg; sodium 777.3mg.
