Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless chicken breast half, cooked and diced
  • 2 cups water
  • 2carrots, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon chicken broth base

Directions

  • Step 1

    • Put cooked chicken meat and water in a large pot and bring to a boil

  • Step 2

    • Add, carrots and garlic and simmer all together for 5 to 10 minutes.

  • Step 3

    • Add the chicken broth and simmer for an additional 5 minutes

Nutriton Facts

Per Servings:50 calories; protein 17.1g; carbohydrates 10.8g; fat 4.6g; cholesterol 41.4mg; sodium 288.5mg.
